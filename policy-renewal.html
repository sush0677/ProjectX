Policy Renewal

Automatic alert when a policy is approaching expiry date

Generate a list of policies (per type) that are expiring within a given period (e.g. within the current month)

Generate renewal notice with respect to policies expiring within a given period with facility to:

  • view
  • amend
  • delete
  • print individual notice
  • print all notices

Support the transmission of the renewal notice by:

  • fax
  • e-mail
  • SMS
  • more than one mode of transmission simultaneously

Ability to define policy renewal models of the insurance companies in the system, so that premium of the renewed policy is automatically calculated

Update claims history of the policy prior to renewal (via manual entry or automatic import)

Ability to override the computed premium

Ability to modify the policy details during renewal

Ability to "black list" a customer (due to claims history, payment history, etc.)
